Cloud Service Providers: Unlocking New Profit Opportunities

Today, numerous businesses are engaged in offering cloud service stacks to their end users. These include major providers like AWS, Google, and Azure, as well as departments within large enterprises that develop proprietary cloud services for internal use.

The IT infrastructure of every Cloud Service Provider (CSP) consists of three primary layers: compute, network, and data storage. Among these, data storage — especially for unstructured data — is often the most complex and challenging to manage.

For CSPs, having a fast and efficient tool to analyze unstructured data and improve data storage efficiency is critical. It not only reduces operational costs but also enables providers to outperform competitors and drive higher revenues.

At Smartstorify, we have introduced a groundbreaking approach to data storage economy, powered by an AI engine that delivers immediate results. Our solution is vendor-agnostic and operates at scale, providing insights across the entire CSP environment while respecting customer data privacy.
